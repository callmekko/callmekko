About Me

I am a freelance web developer located in the Twin Cities of Minnesota. I enjoy solving hard problems and delivering great-looking, smooth-functioning websites.

My principal offering is collaborative sites - content maintained by you, with initial setup and technical support by me. This partnership allows a business owner or organization the opportunity to be agile in their communications with a solid partner on the technical side.

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand. This ensures the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate on, easy to trouble-shoot and accessible.

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build out. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.

Ease Converting Idea to Reality

You can trust me to take a designer's PSD, a model developed with the client, or a major website framework and quickly & accurately convert it into a site that is pixel-perfect match.
